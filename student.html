Discover the support available to you


We know that experiencing mental health difficulties at university can feel overwhelming.

However there are lots of different types of support that might be available to you while at university. We have put this information in one place, so that you can read more about the support programmes that Student Minds offers, as well as services available at your university.

Urgent Support

Are you feeling desperate or distressed right now?

Need to talk to someone?

Samaritans - open every day throughout the year.

Need medical help?

  • If you need medical advice you can book an emergency GP appointment with your GP surgery.

  • If you need urgent help for your mental health get help from 111 online or call 111 and select the mental health option.

    Note: this is a new initiative that came into force on 8th May 2024 which is being rolled out across England as a universal point of access for people experiencing mental health crisis.

  • If you need urgent medical advice call the NHS 111 (England & Wales) or NHS 24(Scotland) - T. 08454 242424
  • If you need immediate medical help or attention call 999 or visit Accident & Emergency (A&E).
